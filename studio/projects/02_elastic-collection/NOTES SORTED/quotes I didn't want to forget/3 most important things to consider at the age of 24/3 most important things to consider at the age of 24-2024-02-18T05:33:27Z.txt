3 most important things to consider at the age of 24

Where you live
Who you're with
What you do

We spend so much time working, or being with people, yet so little deciding. The world agenda will attack us with things to do every single day and we must free up the time to ponder for these big decisions. Spending 1-2 years deciding where you will live the next few years is necessary as it can completely change the trajectory of our lives.
